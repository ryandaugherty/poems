Send a cart for the moon   
oh God   
with the help of bunnies and symboled lions  
I'll trundle light along the bumping dark  
singing love songs   
to an entire world asleep  
  
Until a trumpet wakes, how kind   
how soft the gentle stream set free  
silvery with joy   
to the Sea
